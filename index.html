About Us

Our Mission

Welcome to Healing with Headlines, where we believe in the power of words to heal and inspire. Our mission is to provide uplifting content that promotes mental well-being in a world often dominated by stressful news.


We understand that staying informed is important, but so is maintaining your mental health. That's why we've created tools and resources to help you balance both, transforming your news consumption into an experience that nurtures rather than drains.

Our Services

Podcasts & Interviews

We produce podcast interviews in collaboration with NAMI (National Alliance on Mental Illness) and Navigating for Success Through Wellness to shed light on the effects of toxic media on youth mental health. These episodes featured expert insights and personal stories to educate and empower young listeners.

Fundraisers

We conduct fundraisers to support alliances like NAMI (National Alliance on Mental Illness) to fight towards the common goal of offering mental health resources and making the world a better place.

Workshops and Events

We host interactive sessions designed to build mental health literacy and strengthen emotional resilience, equipping you with the tools to support yourself during challenging times.
